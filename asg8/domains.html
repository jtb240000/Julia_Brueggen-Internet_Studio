Making the Decision

The Discussion

I began the discussion by introducing my ideas and my thoughts about each one. He immediately decided "rethinkrecycling.info" sounded the best, and it was also the option that I was leaning towards the most. Still, we went through each option and evaluated them. He thought "bunnyeatcarrot.info" was a silly option, but it was cute. He thought "rethinkrecycling.info" was a very suitable name for exactly what my website is, though it might not be exactly the right tone for the subtlety I want my website to have, which is similar to my concern that it might sound demanding.
