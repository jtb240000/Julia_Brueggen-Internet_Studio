Domain Name Ideas

  • rethinkrecycling.info

  • therecyclingreality.com

  • everydayeco.info

Making the Decision

The Discussion

I began the discussion by introducing my ideas and my thoughts about each one. He immediately decided "rethinkrecycling.info" sounded the best, and it was also the option that I was leaning towards the most. Still, we went through each option and evaluated them. He thought "bunnyeatcarrot.info" was a silly option, but it was cute. He thought "rethinkrecycling.info" was a very suitable name for exactly what my website is, though it might not be exactly the right tone for the subtlety I want my website to have, which is similar to my concern that it might sound demanding. He agreed that "therecyclingreality.com" sounds more like a news site and he said that claiming to have the "reality" might not be the best approach, although it does match with the purpose of the site to inform about the reality of a situation. Finally, he thought "everydayeco.info" sounded really cool but didn't quite match the topic of the site, which is specifically about recycling and not just general eco-friendliness.

The Chosen Name

In conclusion, the name we both agreed would be best is "rethinkrecycling.info". Even after considering the other options, this one still just sounded like the correct choice for my exact goal with my website. It efficiently communicates the main topic and purpose of the website, and is a catchy name.
