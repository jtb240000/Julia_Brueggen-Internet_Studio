  • From the Better User Experience Through Storytelling Articles: How can framing your project in a story format help you refine your ideas? Write a paragraph explanation.

    • Framing a project in a story format can help designers stay grouded in what they are creating. It helps them single out the themes that they should focus on and gives them a path to follow that is more memorable and engaging. It brings the opportunity for more human connection, because people are more inclined to resonate with a story than with something they just see as a product. Products/services that are based in story are more memorable and meaningful as an experience to users. By framing my website design project in a story format, I can better connect with the persona of my audience and create something that feels more meaningful.

  • From Meet Your Type: A Field Guide to Love and Typography: What are different ways to establish hierarchy (or emphasis) in typography design? Provide a link to a good example of hierarchy.

    • Hierarchy can be established by adding different levels of emphasis to guide the viewer's eye to different parts of the page in an intentional order. Emphasis can be added by increasing size and weight, using eye-catching colors or styles, or placement. The Apple website adds emphasis to their product names that they want people to buy by raising the size and weight and using black text on white background or white text on black background. This shows that the most important part of the page that they want viewers to look at first is the newest products. Other text on the page uses gray color in a smaller size so it is lower on the hierarchy. This is an effective use of hierarchy because they are able to guide the viewer's eye to what is most important to them.
