Bryce's House

Introduction

Bryce's house is located in Texas, in the north Dallas area. It is a two-story house in a suburban area, and is home to Bryce and his family. The location contains all the amenities for extended or overnight stay, though day-trips are the popular choice with most visitors.

Fun Facts

  • Permanent residents of the location include Bryce, his parents, and two siblings, for a total of five human residents.
  • The location is also home to a dog and cats.
