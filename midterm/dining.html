Where to Eat

The Kitchen

The Kitchen is the option with the most variety of food at Bryce's House and is the go-to spot during meal times. It has a bustling and lively atmosphere, with the opportunity to have fun with fellow patrons. Diners can select food from the fridge or even cook for themselves! At certain times, fresh food is cooked and served.

Bryce's Garden

Bryce's Garden is most popular with the bug population, and as a result has declined in popularity with human diners over the past few months. Nevertheless, food options are available, but only for a limited time as they are seasonal. Bryce's Garden currently has two locations, with one serving herbs and the other serving mainly spinach. Currently, lettuce and green beans are no longer available.

Bryce's Room

Bryce's Room serves a limited menu consisting of mixed nuts, dates, and old graham crackers, and an abundance of half-drunk water bottles for drink. In contrast to the menu, the ambiance of the restaurant is unmatched. Comfortable seating and a quiet atmosphere make this restaurant a great choice for a snack break.
