Things to Do

Video Games

Bryce's house contains a substantial amount of infrastructure for video games to be played by locals and visitors. For visitors, video game setups in the living room and in Bryce's room are available, and can be selected based on the video game the visitor would like to play. Commonly requested games are Minecraft and Roblox, but many more are available. For more information on video games or recommendations, contact Bryce!

Homework

If you have something you need to work on, don't hesitate to head to Bryce's house and work on it there! Workspaces are available and the productive ambiance of others also doing their own work around you is an experience you won't want to miss! So don't let your deadlines and obligations prevent you from spending your day at this wonderful location.

Chat with the Locals

This location has a lovely culture which is defined by the people who live there. Interacting with the locals can be a rich learning experience and an oppurtunity to make new friends. Also consider interacting with the animals! The dog is very friendly and smart, and the cats are also friendly.
